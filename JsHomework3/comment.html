How to Make the Best Bubble Tea (Boba Tea)

Bubble tea

Bubble tea is an iced drink that originated in Taiwan in the 1980s. It’s made with tea, milk, sugar, and chewy tapioca balls.

Bubble tea goes by a few names. It’s also known as:

  • Boba tea
  • Boba
  • Boba milk tea
  • Bubble milk tea
  • Pearl milk tea

To drink bubble tea, you need a fat straw that’s big enough to suck up the tapioca balls while drinking the tea.

If you love bubble tea, try making it at home. My recipe tastes just like the real thing! IT’S FREAKING DELICIOUS!

The Secret to My Bubble Tea

I had amazing bubble tea in South Korea and the secret was in the tapioca balls — they were still warm in the iced drink!

The warm tapioca balls were so light and soft and had a nice little chew in the center.

So, in this recipe, I’m doing the same by just cooling down the tapioca balls just a little bit, not cooling them down completely.
